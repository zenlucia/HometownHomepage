My home away from home is Stone, a tiny peaceful canal town in Staffordshire, UK. From Stone Crown Wharf, hop onto a narrow boat, cruise on this canal, soon you will arrive in World of Wedgwood, your first stop, where your relaxing magical adventure begins.

Stone, UK

Top three places to experience near Stone:

Make

World of Wedgwood

World of Wedgewood is a world-class ceramic museum where you can enjoy beautiful display of Wedgwood historical collections, throw a pot or decorate your own plate and enjoy English afternoon tea.

Observe

Monkey Forest

Your next stop is Monkey Forest, the ONLY place in the UK where you can walk amongst 140 free roaming Barbary macaque monkeys!

Seek

Trentham Gardens

Finally you arrive at Trentham Gardens, 725 acres of a spectacular Italian garden, enchanting ancient Woodland and a tranquil lake with over 21 magical fairies dotted around for your to seek.
